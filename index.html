🏠 Home

Yoran Bot is a Discord bot built for Roblox complex systems shop communities. It provides moderation tools, a ticket system, YouTube subscription verification, automatic role management, and announcement utilities.

The bot uses Google OAuth 2.0 (Device Authorization Flow) to verify that users are subscribed to the associated YouTube channel before granting access to exclusive content channels.

🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Yoran Bot ("the Bot", "we", "us") is a Discord bot designed for Roblox systems shop communities. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with the Bot on Discord.

2. Information We Collect

When you use the !verifyyoutube command, we temporarily access:

  • Your Discord user ID — to identify which Discord account to assign a role to
  • Your YouTube account — via Google OAuth 2.0 Device Flow, to verify that you are subscribed to our YouTube channel. We only request read-only access to your YouTube subscription data (youtube.readonly scope)

We do not collect your email address, password, personal name, YouTube watch history, or any other personal data beyond what is strictly necessary for subscription verification.

3. How We Use Your Information

The data collected is used exclusively to:

  • Verify that your YouTube account is subscribed to the designated channel
  • Assign the appropriate Discord role upon successful verification

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any purpose beyond the above.

4. Data Retention

We do not permanently store any personal data. The OAuth access token obtained during verification is used once to check your subscription status and is immediately discarded. No database stores your YouTube credentials or tokens.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. The only external service we communicate with during verification is Google's YouTube Data API v3, governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Google API Services

Yoran Bot's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy , including the Limited Use requirements.

7. Security

All communication with Google's API is performed over HTTPS. Access tokens are never logged or stored permanently.

8. Children's Privacy

Yoran Bot is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this section.
